Membership Level Benefits

NP Membership (Non Profit Organization)

Annual Membership Fee: None ()

Description of NP Membership:

  • Tailored for non-profit organizations dedicated to community betterment. Enjoy the full spectrum of Chamber benefits without any membership fees.

Bronze Membership

Annual membership Fee: R2 000

Benefits of Bronze Membership:

  • Basic listing in the Chamber's online directory.
  • Participation in Chamber networking events.
  • Access to chamber news and business updates in the area.
  • Access to member-exclusive webinars and training sessions.

Silver Membership

Annual membership Fee: R4 000

Benefits of Silzer Membership:

  • Benefits of previous membership.
  • Featured listing in the Chamber's online directory.
  • Priority access to Chamber-sponsored events and workshops.
  • Opportunity to host or sponsor a Chamber event.
  • 5% discount for the following year's membership.

Gold Membership

Annual membership Fee: R6 000

Benefits of Gold Membership:

  • Benefits of previous membership.
  • 10% discount for the following year's membership.
  • Prominent logo placement on the Chamber's website
  • Exclusive access to high-profile Chamber events and VIP networking opportunities.
  • Dedicated marketing support, including social media features and press releases.
  • Direct participation in decisions to improve the Chamber of Commerce in the local area and to implement plans to support smaller businesses.
