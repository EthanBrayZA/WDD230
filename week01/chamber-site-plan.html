Site Name - Cape Town Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Ethan Bray

WDD 230

Overview

Purpose 1

The purpose of our chamber of commerce site is to help the small businesses in the community. The members of the chamber of commerce and the locals will benefit from the website in many ways. The website will help increase the small business owners by getting more exposure and it will help the locals by providing knowledge of the services nearby.

Purpose 2

Another purpose of the website is to increase knowledge in the community and increase the number of attendees to community events. This website will also help the community grow stronger together.

Scenarios 1

What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?

Scenarios 2

Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?

Scenarios 3

What has been the population growth in the area?
